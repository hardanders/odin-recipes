Savory Pork Chops

Ingredients

  • 4 Pork Chops
  • 1 Cup Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Teaspoons Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoon & 1 Teaspoon Ranch Salad Dressing
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1 Dash Pepper
  • 1 Dash Dried Oregano
  • 1 Dash Dried Cilantro
  • 12 Oz Canned Green Beans
  • 1 lb Deli Potato Salad

Steps:

  1. Mix together tomato sauce, Worcestershire sauce, red wine vinegar, Ranch salad dressing, garlic, pepper, oregano and cilantro.
  2. Place pork chops in a shallow pan with a lid. Pour sauce over top.
  3. Heat to boiling, then cover and reduce to lowest temperature.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Uncover and turn burner up to medium.
  6. Place green beans in a saucepan and heat on medium.
  7. Serve with deli potato salad.
