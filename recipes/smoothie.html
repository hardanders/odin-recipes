Green Detox Smoothie

Description

Have you been feeling sluggish lately? Maybe having a hard time climbing out of bed and getting your day going? Well here's a drink you should try out, and it might just turn your life around.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/4 pear, chopped
  • 1/4 green apple, chopped
  • 1/4 avocado, chopped
  • 3 brocolli florets

Steps:

  1. Blend pineapple juice, spinach, pear, apple, avocado, and brocolli florets together in a blender until smooth.
