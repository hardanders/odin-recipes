Dark and Stormy

Description

So you gave the green detox smoothie a try, and it didn't cure your ails? Well perhaps this will be the drink for you - a classic among sailors and seamen. This drink can be highly alcoholic, depending on your ratios, so drink with caution...

Ingredients

  • 2 fl oz dark rum
  • 4 fl oz ginger beer
  • 1/2 cup ice

Steps

  1. Combine rum and ginger beer in an old fashioned glass. Add ice and stir.
